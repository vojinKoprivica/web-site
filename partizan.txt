Fudbalski klub Partizan (Serbian Cyrillic: Фудбалски клуб Партизан, IPA: [fûdbalskiː klûːb partǐzaːn]; English: 
Partizan Football Club) is a Serbian professional football club based in Belgrade. It forms a major part of the Partizan multi-sport club.[2] 
The club plays in the Serbian SuperLiga and has spent its entire history in the top tier of Yugoslav and 
Serbian football having won a total of 45 official trophies.[3] finishing in the Yugoslav league all-time table as second.[4]
